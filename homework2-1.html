Dongseo University

It is a four-year private university located in Jurye-dong, Sasang-gu, Busan.

It is an East-West engineering university under Dongseo Academy, a school corporation established in 1970, and after opening in 1992 (4), it was changed to the current school name in 1996, and at the same time, it has grown into a general university with 7 universities, 3 affiliates, and 2 faculties. The school celebrated its 30th anniversary in 2022.

Currently, a total of 78 departments, 32 humanities and social sciences, 7 natural sciences, 22 arts and sports, and 17 engineering departments are in operation.

In 2024, it was selected as the largest government-funded project ever, the Glocal University 30 project. It was selected as a combined model with Dong-A University and will receive 100 billion won over the next five years.

The founding ideology of this university is to cultivate competent human resources who can complete the character of students and serve God and mankind through proper harmony between various studies and school life based on the Christian spirit.

Based on the founding ideology of the realization of the Christian spirit, we value each person and seek to maximize the self-worth of The Only One by discovering the talents of each student.

The core competencies of Dongseo University students are as follows

  • empathic communication
  • originality

  • Knowledge exploration
  • problem-solving ability
  • Global Mind
  • Understanding Asian Culture
