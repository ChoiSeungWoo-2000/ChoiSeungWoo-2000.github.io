Introduction of Dongseo University Minseok Library

Dongseo University Minseok Library

Dongseo University Library began its history with the opening of Dongseo Institute of Technology in 1992, and has been pursuing user-centered services and smoothly supporting the research of professors and students as a knowledge and information center.

In 1995, our university operated a total library computerization system and opened the nation's first advanced facility Audio & Visual Library to expand the pivotal role of universities and communities.

In addition, the design library was opened in 1999 as part of its specialization, and in 2001, the E-Library of the concept of Education, which combines the functions of education, culture, and rest areas while improving academic information services, presented a new library model.

In September 2007, the Minseok Library, named after the name of Dr. Minseok Jang Sung-man, founder of Dongseo Academy, a school that realizes the noble Christian spirit through education, was opened.
